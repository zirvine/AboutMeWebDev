My story
Click here for contact information

My name is Zach Irvine, and I want to have a profession in Information Systems someday. I grew up in Sandy, Utah with 3 sisters. High School was an amazing experience for me, yet I did not gain any knowledge as to what I wanted to do with my life.

I volunteered 2 years of my life serving a mission in Tahiti. I learned how to work hard and serve others, and while I was in Tahiti, I learned more about myself. I knew I wanted to help others.

I have changed my major several times, others in my life said I should major in business. Once my wife and I heard about the Information Systems major, she told me that that was the major for me.

I love to be outside, hike and run on my free time. I am an avid Jazz fan, and I like playing the piano and writing books too. My greatest passion in life is meeting others and getting to know them.

I know I will be a great asset to any company that hires me, having an enthusiasm for any project I take. I work well with others, and I am honest in everything I do.
