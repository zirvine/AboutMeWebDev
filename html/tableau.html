Profit of State Within the United States

Most states are profitable across the country, with some states having an overall loss, such as Texas, Ohio, and Pennsylvania. California is the most profitable state in the United States, followed by New York and Washington.


California Monthly Profits since January 2013

California, the highest-profit state, has gradually increased in profits in that area, showing fluxuation, but overall growth in profits.


Profit by Product Category in California

California has three product categories: Furniture, Office Supplies, and Technology. Office Supplies boasts the highest profit, followed by Technology, and Furniture comes in last.
